Sevices provided by Urban Development

Every Client is an individual and essential to us; with this in mind we ensure design is distinctive, unique and most importantly cost effective.

Architecture

We know that a “personal bespoke” service is what every Client expects and deserves. We therefore ensure there is only one point of contact within our company.

This contact will be a Senior Manager and will know every detail of the project from conception through to final completion.

Environmental

We want to create, provide, embed and sustain in Clients, a deliberate Environmental Culture in all Building and Energy installations, Energy conservation and sustainability are foremost in our design delivery. A life cycle design approach is key to our business philosophy.

We are committed to establishing a detailed understanding of the client requirements at an early stage and provide the resources and research to find the best practice solutions to meet them.

Design Management

On projects where we have been appointed as Multi-Disciplinary consultants, a crucial service that is always included is “Design Management”.

This involves co-ordination exercises between all the disciplines to ensure that the design is fully compliant to the Client brief, meets all Regulations and Standards and most importantly is within the Clients budget.
